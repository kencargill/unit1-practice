My name is Ken. I'm a hydroponic farmer that used to be a baker. I have an art degree and prefer to make things. I like to knit, take care of sheep and goats, and camp. I have a dog named Teddy and a cat named Pirate.

How I got my start

I first got interested in coding through social media websites in the early 2000s and 2010s. I felt unsure of how to teach myself to code and quickly got distracted by other pursuits. Namely, knitting. I got reintroduced to coding through some programmer friends in the past few years and through my knitting machine. Both of these things made coding approachable for me again and I started playing around with Codecademy and freecodecamp. The farm I currently work on uses a lot of software to control and monitor the farm, this also brought me back to my interest in coding.

I was introduced to Grand Circus by a close friend of mine who had attended in 2019. I heard a lot about his success and appreciation for the program and decided to give it a shot myself.
